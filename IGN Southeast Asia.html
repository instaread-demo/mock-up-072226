CookieRun: OvenSmash Turns The Franchise Into A Real-Time PvP Arena

After more than a decade of side-scrolling runners and RPG spin-offs, CookieRun is heading in a very different direction.

With CookieRun: OvenSmash, developer Devsisters is introducing the franchise’s first real-time PvP action experience, a shift that feels less like a spin-off and more like a reset.

Set in Platter City, a new urban fantasy hub that brings together characters from across the series, OvenSmash expands the CookieRun universe with new factions, arenas, and original characters alongside familiar faces like GingerBrave.

At its core, OvenSmash is built around short, high-intensity matches that last up to three minutes.

It’s a format that’s clearly designed for quick sessions, but still leaves room for competitive depth.

Players can choose from eight roles — including Mage, Tank, Marksman, and Assassin — each offering distinct playstyles that encourage team coordination and strategic positioning. Rather than focusing on long-term grinding, the game leans heavily into mechanical skill and decision-making, with ranking systems that reward performance over time invested.

One of OvenSmash’s defining ideas is its emphasis on outplaying opponents rather than out-levelling them.

Matches move quickly, and there’s little room for passive play. Success comes down to timing, awareness, and how well teams execute under pressure.

Adding to that layer of strategy are Spell Cards, which act as game-changing abilities capable of turning the tide in seconds. It’s the kind of system that could open the door to evolving metas, especially as players experiment with different combinations.

The game also features multiple modes, including Castle Crasher, Wild Royale, and Gnome Battle, offering a mix of structured and chaotic gameplay that caters to both competitive players and more casual sessions.

Here’s some good news for you! Pre-registration is now live, with early rewards already up for grabs.

Published in Southeast Asia, Taiwan, and Hong Kong by VNGGames, CookieRun: OvenSmash is now open for pre-registration on iOS and Android.

Players who sign up early can earn in-game rewards, including Coins, Crystals, and exclusive cosmetics. Community milestones will unlock additional bonuses, with larger rewards tied to total sign-up numbers — including the Summer Spirit Set skin at one million registrations.

There are also limited physical prizes available for selected participants, such as themed accessories and collectables tied to the game’s launch campaign.

Players looking to secure early rewards and stay updated on launch details can access the official channels below:
