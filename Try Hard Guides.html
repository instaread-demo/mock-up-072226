Harmonies Tips & Strategies

  • Look for Easy Wins First – Start by finding the most obvious group. Locking in one set helps eliminate possibilities.
  • Don’t Be Fooled by Red Herrings – Harmonies loves to throw in words that almost fit multiple themes. Trust your gut, but double-check before locking in a group.
  • Think Outside the Box – Some connections are about sounds, spellings, or pop culture references — not just categories. Stay flexible.
  • Use Process of Elimination – If you’re down to 8 words and two groups left, try mixing and matching to see what clicks.
  • Practice Makes Patterns – The more you play, the more you’ll recognize how Harmonies structures its themes — and how tricky it likes to be.
