Stone Age Survival ‘Polylithic’ Leaves Steam Early Access Late August

Today, developer polyperfect and publisher indie.io announced that their “colourful” Stone Age survival title ‘Polylithic’ will exit Steam Early Access and launch into a full 1.0 release on 25 August on PC—with it’s most substantial update yet.

Set in a vibrant, low-poly world where humans are far from the apex of the food chain, Polylithic casts players as tribe leaders navigating the Stone Age from Paleolithic hunter-gatherer to Neolithic settler, with online co-op for up to 10 players and an infinitely generated Sandbox Mode for those who prefer to skip the instructions and cause problems.

Since launching into Early Access in November 2023, Polylithic has shipped more than 50 updates shaped by direct player feedback, expanding from a solo hunter-gatherer experience into a full tribe-management survival game with online co-op for up to 10 players, a procedurally generated Sandbox Mode, new biomes and a continuously deepening tech tree. In early 2026, Novák announced a deliberate creative pivot — leaning into humor, visual personality and a new cutscene style inspired by BBC nature documentaries and the deadpan narration of David Attenborough,  a shift that now defines the 1.0 experience.

Polylithic 1.0 is a significant expansion of everything the Early Access build established. Players can now customize their character (it’s the Stone Age, somehow), manage a new hunger and body system, adopt a best friend as a dog or hyena companion, emote around the campfire, yell at wildlife, contend with enemy tribes and attempt the new cooking minigame, which generates recipes at random, with predictably chaotic results. The update also brings new story moments, smarter AI, new items, weapons and mushrooms, a major optimization pass and so much more.

“Polylithic 1.0 is not the game I originally set out to make, and I mean that in the best way. Early Access changed it. The community changed it. At some point I realized the game I actually wanted to make was, more chaotic and a lot more willing to make you smile while a pack of hyenas chases you through a bog. That’s what 1.0 is. We built something I’m genuinely proud of, and I can’t wait for people to ruin prehistory with it.” — Pavel Novák, Producer and Art Director, polyperfect

Polylithic launches out of Early Access on August 25, 2026, on Steam, priced at $14.99 with a 35% launch discount. The game supports English, Czech, French, German, and Russian, and has added Spanish, Brazilian Portuguese, Chinese, and Japanese support for its 1.0.
