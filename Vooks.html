It’s that time of year again. We’re freezing our butts off, so there’s no better time to stay inside and play some games. There’s also no better time to buy some games, with Nintendo’s Winter Sale now on across both the Switch and Switch 2.

While there are just 64 games on sale for the Switch 2, many of them are discounted for the first time, with even more hitting their all-time lowest prices.

The Switch, though, is another kettle of fish. There are over 2,000 games discounted, an absolutely ludicrous amount, and yep, they’re all there below for your table-sorting pleasure. Normally we’d highlight a few standout deals, but there are simply too many to process this time.
